Wheel to Surf

Coastal Adaptive Sports hosts a variety of events throughout the year that empower individuals with disabilities to become active in sports. Wheel to Surf is one of their most notable projects. At these gatherings, volunteers make surfing accessible and inclusive for all.

Alice is passionate about capturing the joy at Wheel to Surf. From Myrtle Beach to Folly Beach, she's volunteered at numerous events to ensure every participant and their families leave with lasting photographic memories of their time in the waves.

Learn more about becoming a participant or volunteer with Coastal Adaptive Sports.
